// ABOUT THE STUDIO

Giovanni Filardi (b. 1997, Italy) is a Lead Industrial Designer whose practice is strictly governed by reductive logic. Operating from Turin, the studio focuses on the intersection between uncompromising aesthetic purity and the brutal reality of high-volume mass production.

Having studied the complexities of international manufacturing lines, the studio’s mandate is not to engage in theoretical styling, but to force raw materials into market-ready physical assets.
By eradicating every non-essential element, we translate complex engineering constraints into objects of absolute structural permanence.

To date, the studio has successfully engineered over 35 mass-produced products currently on the global market. Partnering with international brands, we oversee the entire industrial lifecycle, ensuring that the initial architectural vision is flawlessly replicated at scale. This rigorous approach has been validated by consecutive official recognitions at the [d]arc awards (2024, 2025) // widely considered the Oscars of the lighting design industry.
